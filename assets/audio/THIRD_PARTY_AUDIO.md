# Third-party audio

All bundled sound effects are released under Creative Commons Zero (CC0).

- `vehicle/brake_skid.wav`, `ui/menu_move.wav`, `ui/menu_select.wav`: selected files from “Various Sound Effects” by Spring Spring, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/various-sound-effects-0
- `engine/4age_loop.mp3`: “4A-GE Engine Loop 2” by qopsinonstudios, Freesound. https://freesound.org/people/qopsinonstudios/sounds/509627/
- `engine/sedan_loop.mp3`: “Sedan engine loop” by Dmitry_mansurev64, Freesound. https://freesound.org/people/Dmitry_mansurev64/sounds/748027/
- `engine/v8_rev.mp3`: “v8 engine rev.wav” by overmedium, Freesound. https://freesound.org/people/overmedium/sounds/651534/
- `vehicle/metal_scrape.mp3`: “Metal Scrape” by magnuswaker, Freesound. https://freesound.org/people/magnuswaker/sounds/530075/
- `impacts/vehicle_collision.mp3`: “Collision” by qubodup, Freesound. https://freesound.org/people/qubodup/sounds/332058/
- `impacts/car_crash_heavy.mp3`: “Car Crash (with Glass)” by magnuswaker, Freesound. https://freesound.org/people/magnuswaker/sounds/592388/
- `ui/powerup_short.wav`: “Gem collect SFX” by Bobjt, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/gem-collect-sfx

- `engine/engine_*.wav`: “racing car engine sound loops” by domasx2, OpenGameArt. Six pitch variants derived from public-domain source audio. https://opengameart.org/content/racing-car-engine-sound-loops
- `vehicle/wall_scrape.ogg`: `scrape-4.ogg` from “Scrapes” by AntumDeluge, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/scrapes
- `impacts/metal_thud_*.wav`: “Metal Impact Sounds” by BMacZero, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/metal-impact-sounds

Attribution is not required by CC0, but the source list is retained for provenance and future replacement.
