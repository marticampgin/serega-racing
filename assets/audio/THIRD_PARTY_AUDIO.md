# Third-party audio

All bundled sound effects are released under Creative Commons Zero (CC0).

- `engine/engine_*.wav`: “racing car engine sound loops” by domasx2, OpenGameArt. Six pitch variants derived from public-domain source audio. https://opengameart.org/content/racing-car-engine-sound-loops
- `impacts/crash.ogg`: “Crash Collision” by qubodup, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/crash-collision
- `impacts/metal_thud_*.wav`: “Metal Impact Sounds” by BMacZero, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/metal-impact-sounds
- `vehicle/wall_scrape.ogg`: `scrape-4.ogg` from “Scrapes” by AntumDeluge, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/scrapes
- `vehicle/brake_skid.wav`, `ui/powerup.wav`, `ui/menu_move.wav`, `ui/menu_select.wav`: selected files from “Various Sound Effects” by Spring Spring, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/various-sound-effects-0

Attribution is not required by CC0, but the source list is retained for provenance and future replacement.
