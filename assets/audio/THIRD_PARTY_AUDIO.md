# Audio provenance

The engine, impact, scrape, sideswipe, brake, and tire sounds in `engine/`,
`impacts/`, and `vehicle/` were generated by the project owner with ElevenLabs
and supplied specifically for this project.

The `engine/*_loop.wav` files are one-second raised-cosine loop derivatives of
the corresponding owner-supplied recordings. They can be regenerated with
`tools/build_seamless_audio_loop.ps1`.

The remaining bundled CC0 interface sounds are:

- `ui/menu_move.wav` and `ui/menu_select.wav`: selected files from "Various Sound Effects" by Spring Spring, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/various-sound-effects-0
- `ui/powerup_short.wav`: "Gem collect SFX" by Bobjt, OpenGameArt. https://opengameart.org/content/gem-collect-sfx

Attribution is not required by CC0, but the source list is retained for provenance.
